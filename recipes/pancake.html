Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 gm whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider
  • 1/4 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 120 ml almond milk
  • 1/4 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon flax seeds
  • 120 ml water
  1. Begin with preparing the pancake batter
  2. Mix the wet and dry Ingredients
  3. Carefully pour the batter on a pan, let it cook and serve
